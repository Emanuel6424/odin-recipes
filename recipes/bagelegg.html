Egg Bagel Sandwitch

Prefect for a quick breakfast

Will have you wanting to make another one before you leav for work

Ingredients

  • 1 Bagel
  • 1 Egg
  • Cream Cheese
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Hot scaue

Driections:

  • 1. Cut bagel in half and cook in the toast until golden brown
  • 2. Cook egg to preference and put shredded cheese on top
  • 3. Once bagel is cooked, slap on some cream cheese
  • 4. Assemble and put hot scause on it and enjoy ;)
